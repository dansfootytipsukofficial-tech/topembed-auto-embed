### Step 1: Get Your Own Copy
Click the **"Use this template"** button at the top of this page (or Fork this repository)

### Step 2: Enable Automation
1. Go to the **Actions** tab and click "I understand my workflows, go ahead and enable them"
2. Go to **Settings** > **Pages** 
3. Under "Source", select **gh-pages** branch and click **Save**
4. Wait 5 minutes for the first workflow to run

### Step 3: Add to Weebly
1. Copy your GitHub Pages URL: `https://[your-username].github.io/[repo-name]/embed/`
2. In Weebly, drag an **Embed Code** element to your page
3. Paste this code (replace with your URL):

```html
<iframe src="https://[your-username].github.io/[repo-name]/embed/" 
        width="100%" height="900" style="border:0;" allowfullscreen>
</iframe>
```
